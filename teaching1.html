YOU WERE BARABBAS

The True Meaning of Repentance

You are not struggling because you are weak... You are struggling because you have not yet seen.

There was a man named Barabbas. A criminal. Guilty. Condemned.

That day, he was meant to die.

But the crowd shouted: “Release Barabbas!” and “Crucify Jesus!”

Barabbas was set free... because Jesus took his place.

The punishment was transferred. The guilt was transferred. The death was transferred.

This story is about you.

You are Barabbas.

Repentance is not just saying sorry. Repentance is seeing that the cross was meant for you.

When you see it, your heart changes.

Not by force… but by love.

You were the condemned one… but now you are free.
